# Article Notes: AI-Assisted Decision Support and Human Judgment

This article examines how artificial intelligence can help people gather evidence, compare options, detect patterns, forecast outcomes, summarize uncertainty, and monitor decisions while also creating risks of automation bias, opacity, overreliance, deskilling, accountability gaps, and institutional misuse.

Core themes:
- AI support versus automated decision-making
- human judgment and accountable authority
- use-case risk classification
- model uncertainty and limits
- automation bias and overreliance
- explainability and contestability
- fairness and distributional impact
- monitoring and drift
- decision records
- governance and corrective authority
