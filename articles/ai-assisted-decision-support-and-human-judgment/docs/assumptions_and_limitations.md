# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic data and simplified dynamics.

Limitations:
- synthetic AI support designs
- simplified model-confidence and uncertainty dynamics
- simplified automation-bias and oversight scoring
- simplified fairness-risk and contestability indicators
- simplified accountability modeling
- no empirical validation
- no legal, compliance, AI governance, technical, safety, healthcare, finance, public-sector, hiring, education, or regulatory advice

Professional use requires qualified AI governance, legal, compliance, domain, security, privacy, ethics, audit, technical, stakeholder, and public-interest review.
