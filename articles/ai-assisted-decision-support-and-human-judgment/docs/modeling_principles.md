# Modeling Principles

## AI should support judgment, not replace responsibility

Model outputs should be treated as evidence requiring interpretation, not final authority.

## Human oversight must be meaningful

Oversight requires authority, time, training, information, independence, override rights, and institutional support.

## Uncertainty should be visible

Outputs should show uncertainty, missing data, limits, confidence, calibration, context mismatch, and review triggers.

## Contestability connects explanation to accountability

Affected people need practical pathways to challenge, appeal, correct, or obtain human review.

## Monitoring is continuous

AI-supported decision systems can drift because data, users, vendors, policies, and operating contexts change.
