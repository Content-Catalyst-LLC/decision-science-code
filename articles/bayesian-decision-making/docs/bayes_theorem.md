# Bayes' Theorem

Bayes' theorem updates a prior probability after evidence is observed:

P(H | E) = P(E | H)P(H) / P(E)

For a binary hypothesis:

P(H | E) = P(E | H)P(H) / [P(E | H)P(H) + P(E | not H)P(not H)]

The posterior depends on the prior, the likelihood under the hypothesis, and the likelihood under the alternative.
