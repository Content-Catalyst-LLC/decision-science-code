# Article Notes: Behavioral Decision Theory

This article explains how real people and institutions make choices under uncertainty, framing, bounded rationality, salience, loss aversion, social influence, and cognitive constraint.

Core themes:
- behavioral decision theory
- prospect theory
- expected utility
- reference dependence
- loss aversion
- probability weighting
- framing effects
- heuristics and biases
- bounded rationality
- decision architecture
- ethical choice architecture
- decision records
