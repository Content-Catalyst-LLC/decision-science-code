# Article Notes: Cascading Risk and Systemic Decision Failure

This article explains how local disruptions, flawed assumptions, fragile dependencies, and poorly timed interventions can propagate across connected systems until ordinary decision problems become systemic failures.

Core themes:
- cascading risk
- systemic decision failure
- network exposure
- dependency mapping
- feedback amplification
- threshold failure
- common-mode risk
- brittle efficiency
- buffer depletion
- early warning
- containment
- governance and accountability
