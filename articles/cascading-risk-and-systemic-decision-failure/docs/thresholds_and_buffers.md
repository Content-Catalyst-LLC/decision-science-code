# Thresholds and Buffers

Cascades often accelerate when stress crosses thresholds and buffer capacity is depleted.

Indicators:
- buffer depletion
- rising recovery time
- increasing correlation
- near-miss frequency
- workaround dependence
- escalating local failures
- slower restoration after disturbance

Buffers include slack capacity, reserves, redundancy, backup pathways, trained staff, stored inventory, and institutional trust.
