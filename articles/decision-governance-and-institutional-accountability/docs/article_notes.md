# Article Notes: Decision Governance and Institutional Accountability

This article examines how institutions design decision systems that make authority visible, responsibility traceable, judgment reviewable, and learning possible.

Core themes:
- decision governance as institutional architecture
- decision rights and role clarity
- accountability chains
- responsibility gaps
- evidence standards
- decision records
- institutional memory
- review and escalation
- independent challenge
- internal control and assurance
- monitoring and corrective action
- governance learning
