# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic data and simplified dynamics.

Limitations:
- synthetic governance designs
- simplified accountability and responsibility-gap scoring
- simplified review and internal-control modeling
- simplified risk and process-burden representation
- simplified institutional-learning dynamics
- no empirical validation
- no legal, audit, compliance, governance, public-sector, management, board, risk, regulatory, or professional advice

Professional use requires qualified legal, audit, governance, risk, ethics, compliance, operational, technical, public-interest, and domain review.
