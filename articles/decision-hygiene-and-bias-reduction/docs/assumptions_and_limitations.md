# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic decision cases and stylized hygiene effects.

Limitations:
- synthetic judgments
- synthetic outcomes
- simplified bias sources
- simplified noise estimates
- simplified calibration bins
- simplified hygiene effects
- no empirical validation
- no psychological assessment
- no legal, clinical, financial, engineering, policy, organizational, or safety advice

Professional use requires domain expertise, validated data, governance, human review, and careful interpretation.
