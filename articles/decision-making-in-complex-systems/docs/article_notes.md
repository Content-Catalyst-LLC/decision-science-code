# Article Notes: Decision-Making in Complex Systems

This article explains how choices are made inside systems shaped by interdependence, feedback loops, nonlinear effects, adaptation, delays, and emergence.

Core themes:
- interdependence
- feedback loops
- delays
- nonlinearity
- emergence
- adaptation
- threshold risk
- cascading effects
- robust decision-making
- adaptive decision pathways
- systems modeling
- governance and accountability
