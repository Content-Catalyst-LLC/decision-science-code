# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic strategy performance data and stylized structural uncertainty shocks.

Limitations:
- synthetic data
- simplified scenario space
- simplified ambiguity profiles
- simplified regret and robustness scoring
- simplified adaptive simulation
- no empirical validation
- no legal, financial, engineering, climate, medical, policy, organizational, or safety advice

Professional use requires domain expertise, validated evidence, stakeholder review, governance, and careful interpretation.
