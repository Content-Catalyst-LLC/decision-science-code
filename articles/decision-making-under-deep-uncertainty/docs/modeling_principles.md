# Modeling Principles

## Deep uncertainty is structural

Deep uncertainty occurs when decision-makers cannot reliably agree on models, probabilities, values, boundaries, or future states.

## Forecasts become exploratory inputs

Under deep uncertainty, forecasts should support exploration, stress testing, and vulnerability discovery rather than false precision.

## Robustness is a decision criterion

Strategies should be evaluated by performance across many plausible futures, not only by one expected outcome.

## Values and thresholds must be explicit

Performance thresholds, acceptable regret, and robustness criteria are value judgments and should be documented.

## Adaptation requires governance

Adaptive plans need monitoring signals, triggers, review owners, and authority to revise the strategy.
