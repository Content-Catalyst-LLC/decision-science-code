# Article Notes: Decision Quality and the Architecture of Judgment

This article distinguishes decision quality from outcome quality and treats decision quality as an inspectable architecture of judgment.

Core themes:
- decision quality as process standard
- outcome bias and hindsight bias
- architecture of judgment
- framing and alternative quality
- evidence and uncertainty
- trade-off clarity
- behavioral safeguards
- systems consequences
- decision rights and accountability
- learning, review, and decision records
