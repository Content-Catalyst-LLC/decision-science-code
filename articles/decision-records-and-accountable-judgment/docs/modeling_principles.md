# Modeling Principles

## Accountable judgment requires traceability

A decision should be explainable through its frame, alternatives, evidence, assumptions, uncertainty, criteria, trade-offs, dissent, rationale, and monitoring plan.

## Records should be reviewable

Assumptions should be connected to indicators and triggers. The record should tell future reviewers what evidence would require revision.

## Dissent is evidence

Credible dissent can reveal model weaknesses, value conflicts, stakeholder harm, timing issues, or implementation risk.

## Documentation must remain usable

A decision record should preserve the logic of judgment without becoming so complex that it cannot be maintained.
