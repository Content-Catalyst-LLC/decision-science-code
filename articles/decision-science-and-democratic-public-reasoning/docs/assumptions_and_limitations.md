# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic data and simplified dynamics.

Limitations:
- synthetic democratic decision processes
- simplified public-trust and legitimacy dynamics
- simplified participation, contestability, and equity indicators
- simplified uncertainty communication and harm-signal modeling
- simplified public decision records
- no empirical validation
- no legal, public policy, government, regulatory, electoral, public-participation, governance, or civic-process advice

Professional use requires qualified public-sector, legal, governance, community, civic-participation, ethics, data, policy, and domain review.
