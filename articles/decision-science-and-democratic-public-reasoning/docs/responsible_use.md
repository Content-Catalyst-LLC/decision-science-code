# Responsible Use

These workflows are decision-support scaffolds.

Use them to:
- compare democratic decision processes
- structure public reasoning records
- evaluate participation quality
- examine contestability and legitimacy
- monitor public trust and harm signals
- test review triggers
- support discussion and learning

Do not use them as a substitute for public law, democratic accountability, community engagement, public-sector expertise, legal review, civic-process design, or professional judgment.
