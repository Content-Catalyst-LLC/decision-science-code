# AI Governance Decisions

AI governance decisions include:
- approve, reject, pause, or retire an AI use case
- build, buy, adapt, or prohibit a system
- assign risk tier
- define evidence requirements
- set human oversight level
- define allowed and prohibited uses
- approve vendor terms
- set monitoring indicators
- respond to drift or incidents
- publish disclosures or reports
- provide appeal and contestability pathways

A strong AI governance decision is evidence-based, risk-aware, rights-aware, accountable, monitored, and revisable.
