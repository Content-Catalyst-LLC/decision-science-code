# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic data and simplified dynamics.

Limitations:
- synthetic governance options
- simplified risk scoring
- simplified evidence, oversight, fairness, transparency, and security measures
- simplified drift and incident simulation
- simplified vendor and institutional capacity representation
- no empirical validation
- no legal, compliance, procurement, cybersecurity, privacy, employment, healthcare, financial, public-sector, or regulatory advice

Professional use requires qualified legal, technical, governance, ethics, security, privacy, procurement, domain, and affected-community review.
