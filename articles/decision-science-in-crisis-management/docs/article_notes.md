# Article Notes: Decision Science in Crisis Management

This article examines how institutions make urgent, high-stakes decisions under uncertainty, time pressure, incomplete information, operational stress, public scrutiny, and cascading risk.

Core themes:
- crisis management as a decision system
- sensemaking under uncertainty
- risk triage and prioritization
- incident command and coordination
- situational awareness
- public trust and crisis communication
- ethics and distributional burdens
- cascading risk
- continuity and resilience
- adaptive response
- after-action learning
