# Article Notes: Decision Science in Financial Risk Management

This article examines how institutions make high-stakes judgments under uncertainty when capital, liquidity, solvency, regulation, incentives, behavior, model risk, and systemic interdependence are all in play.

Core themes:
- portfolio stress testing
- regime analysis
- capital resilience
- liquidity pressure
- tail shocks
- model risk
- risk appetite
- systemic risk
- governance and risk culture
- behavioral finance
- AI model governance
- climate and long-horizon financial risk
- decision records
