# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic data and simplified dynamics.

Limitations:
- synthetic data
- simplified portfolio stress profiles
- simplified capital path simulation
- simplified liquidity dynamics
- simplified model-risk review
- simplified governance scoring
- no empirical validation
- no investment, trading, legal, accounting, tax, insurance, regulatory, supervisory, banking, or financial advice

Professional use requires qualified financial, risk, compliance, legal, accounting, regulatory, actuarial, and governance expertise with validated data and institutional review.
