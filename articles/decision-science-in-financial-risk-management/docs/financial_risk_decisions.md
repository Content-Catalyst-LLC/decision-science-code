# Financial Risk Decisions

Financial risk decisions include:
- exposure acceptance
- portfolio allocation
- loan concentration
- hedging
- liquidity buffers
- funding mix
- capital planning
- model approval
- management overlays
- stress-test response
- AI model governance
- supervisory response

A strong financial risk decision must be analytically rigorous, liquidity-aware, model-aware, incentive-aware, and governance-ready.
