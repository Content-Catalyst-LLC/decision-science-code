# Article Notes: Decision Science in Healthcare

This article examines how structured judgment, probabilistic reasoning, evidence synthesis, behavioral insight, systems thinking, ethical analysis, and operational modeling shape decisions across clinical care, health systems, and public health policy.

Core themes:
- clinical uncertainty
- probabilistic diagnosis
- treatment decision analysis
- shared decision-making
- cost-effectiveness
- hospital operations
- queue pressure
- patient safety
- health equity
- public health policy
- AI decision support
- decision records
