# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic data and simplified dynamics.

Limitations:
- synthetic data
- simplified treatment scoring
- simplified diagnostic probability examples
- simplified cost-effectiveness examples
- simplified hospital-capacity model
- simplified patient safety and surge response
- no empirical validation
- no medical, clinical, legal, financial, insurance, regulatory, operational, public-health, or safety advice

Professional use requires qualified clinical expertise, validated data, patient involvement, ethics review, institutional governance, and careful interpretation.
