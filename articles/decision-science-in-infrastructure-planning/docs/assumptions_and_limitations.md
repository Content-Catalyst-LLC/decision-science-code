# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic data and simplified dynamics.

Limitations:
- synthetic infrastructure alternatives
- simplified scenario values
- simplified lifecycle and resilience scoring
- simplified asset deterioration dynamics
- simplified hazard exposure and service reliability
- simplified equity, environmental, and feasibility scores
- no empirical validation
- no engineering, legal, investment, construction, procurement, environmental, public-finance, planning, safety, or policy advice

Professional use requires qualified engineering, planning, finance, legal, environmental, public engagement, and governance expertise with validated data and institutional review.
