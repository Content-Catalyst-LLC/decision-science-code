# Infrastructure Decisions

Infrastructure decisions include:
- maintaining existing assets
- retrofitting or rehabilitating assets
- expanding capacity
- redesigning services
- investing in nature-based solutions
- staging modular upgrades
- relocating or retreating from exposed areas
- building redundancy
- protecting critical services
- financing and procurement choices
- monitoring and adaptation pathways

A strong infrastructure decision must be technically sound, fiscally responsible, publicly legitimate, equitable, resilient, and revisable.
