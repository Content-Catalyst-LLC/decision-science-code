# Article Notes: Decision Science in Organizational Strategy

This article examines how organizations make consequential choices under uncertainty when competition, capability, cognition, timing, governance, incentives, resources, and institutional constraints interact.

Core themes:
- strategy as a decision system
- bounded rationality
- uncertainty classification
- strategic alternatives
- resource allocation
- dynamic capabilities
- behavioral strategy
- systems thinking
- governance and incentives
- AI decision support
- strategic learning
- decision records
