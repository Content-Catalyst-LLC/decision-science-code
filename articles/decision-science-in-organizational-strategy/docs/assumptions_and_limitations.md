# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic data and simplified dynamics.

Limitations:
- synthetic strategy profiles
- simplified scenario evaluation
- simplified robust scoring
- simplified assumption-drift dynamics
- simplified capability and governance measures
- no empirical validation
- no legal, financial, investment, management, organizational, employment, regulatory, or strategic advice

Professional use requires validated data, organizational context, executive judgment, stakeholder review, governance review, and careful interpretation.
