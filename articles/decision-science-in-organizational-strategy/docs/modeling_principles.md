# Modeling Principles

## Strategy is a decision system

A strategy becomes real through repeated decisions about attention, resources, capabilities, governance, incentives, trade-offs, and learning.

## Strategic alternatives matter

A sophisticated scoring method cannot rescue a weak option set. Strong strategy requires genuine alternatives.

## Uncertainty classification matters

Risk, uncertainty, ambiguity, deep uncertainty, and strategic surprise require different decision methods.

## Capabilities and governance shape feasibility

Strategic ambition does not equal execution capacity. Capability fit and governance feasibility are part of the decision.

## Accountability requires records

Decision records preserve assumptions, alternatives, dissent, trade-offs, commitments, and revision triggers.
