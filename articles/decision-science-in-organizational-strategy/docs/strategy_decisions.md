# Strategy Decisions

Organizational strategy decisions include:
- market entry
- repositioning
- capability renewal
- business-model transformation
- innovation portfolios
- mergers and acquisitions
- organizational redesign
- AI strategy
- resilience investment
- partnership or exit
- governance redesign

A strong strategic decision must be coherent, resourced, uncertainty-aware, behaviorally realistic, system-sensitive, governance-aligned, and revisable.
