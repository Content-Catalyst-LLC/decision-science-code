# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic data and simplified dynamics.

Limitations:
- synthetic data
- simplified multi-objective scoring
- simplified scenario robustness
- simplified implementation-drift model
- simplified trust and uptake dynamics
- no empirical validation
- no legal, financial, medical, public-administration, public-policy, regulatory, or safety advice

Professional use requires domain expertise, validated data, stakeholder review, legal review, institutional governance, and careful interpretation.
