# Responsible Use

These workflows are decision-support scaffolds.

Use them to:
- compare policy packages across objectives
- examine robustness across scenarios
- flag equity, legitimacy, and implementation-capacity concerns
- simulate public trust, uptake, feedback, and implementation drift
- document public policy decision records
- support transparent public reasoning

Do not use them to automate policy decisions, manufacture certainty, suppress dissent, hide value judgments, bypass public deliberation, or replace accountable human judgment.
