# Article Notes: Decision Science in Sustainability

This article examines how structured judgment, systems thinking, evidence, uncertainty, behavioral insight, ecological limits, public values, and long-term responsibility shape decisions that affect environmental, social, and economic systems.

Core themes:
- sustainability decision science
- multi-objective trade-offs
- ecological thresholds
- environmental justice
- systems thinking
- robust decision-making
- adaptive pathways
- resource pressure
- implementation capacity
- governance and accountability
- decision records
