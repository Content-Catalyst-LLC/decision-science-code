# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic data and simplified dynamics.

Limitations:
- synthetic data
- simplified multi-objective scoring
- simplified resource pressure model
- simplified adaptive response model
- simplified scenario performance
- simplified threshold review
- no empirical validation
- no legal, financial, engineering, environmental, climate, ecological, public-policy, or safety advice

Professional use requires domain expertise, validated data, stakeholder review, environmental analysis, legal review, institutional governance, and careful interpretation.
