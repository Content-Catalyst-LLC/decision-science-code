# Justice and Equity

Sustainability decisions distribute risks and benefits across people, places, ecosystems, and generations.

Justice questions:
- Who is exposed to environmental harm?
- Who benefits from the intervention?
- Who pays transition costs?
- Who has voice?
- What risks are passed forward?
- What cannot be traded away?

Equity is part of the decision problem because sustainability asks what kind of future is preserved, for whom, and at whose expense.
