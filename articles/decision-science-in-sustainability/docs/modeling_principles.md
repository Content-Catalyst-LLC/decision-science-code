# Modeling Principles

## Sustainability is multi-objective

Sustainability decisions balance environmental outcomes, social outcomes, economic feasibility, governance capacity, resilience, justice, and long-term viability.

## Thresholds matter

Some ecological limits should function as constraints, safe minimum standards, or precautionary triggers rather than ordinary preferences.

## Systems respond

Policies change incentives, behavior, markets, infrastructure, institutions, and ecological pressure.

## Future uncertainty is structural

Sustainability strategies should be tested across scenarios and designed with adaptive review.

## Accountability requires records

Decision records preserve assumptions, trade-offs, thresholds, stakeholder concerns, monitoring indicators, and revision triggers.
