# Article Notes: Forecasting and Decision Support

This article explains how forecasting supports decision science when forecasts are connected to alternatives, thresholds, consequences, uncertainty, monitoring, and accountable records.

Core themes:
- probabilistic forecasting
- forecast accuracy
- calibration
- forecast horizons
- decision thresholds
- value of information
- base rates
- reference classes
- model risk
- data drift
- early-warning signals
- scenario comparison
- forecast dashboards
- AI-assisted forecasting
