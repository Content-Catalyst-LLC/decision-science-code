# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic forecast data and stylized decision-support diagnostics.

Limitations:
- synthetic forecasts
- synthetic outcomes
- simplified expected-loss logic
- simplified threshold rules
- no empirical validation
- no domain-specific deployment guidance
- no legal, financial, clinical, public-policy, engineering, or safety advice

Professional use requires domain expertise, validated data, calibration review, subgroup auditing, governance, and accountable decision processes.
