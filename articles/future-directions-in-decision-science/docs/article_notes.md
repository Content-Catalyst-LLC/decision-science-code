# Article Notes: Future Directions in Decision Science

This article examines how decision science is evolving as institutions face deeper uncertainty, artificial intelligence, democratic legitimacy challenges, complex systems, climate risk, infrastructure fragility, algorithmic governance, geopolitical instability, organizational accountability, and long-term public consequences.

Core themes:
- decision systems
- AI-assisted decision intelligence
- governance and accountability infrastructure
- adaptive and robust decision systems
- participatory and democratic decision science
- complex systems and cascading risk
- digital twins and simulation
- reproducible computational workflows
- ethics, equity, and power
- uncertainty communication and epistemic humility
