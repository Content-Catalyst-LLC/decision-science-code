# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic data and simplified dynamics.

Limitations:
- synthetic future decision-science pathways
- simplified AI-support and governance maturity scoring
- simplified uncertainty, systems-awareness, ethics, and adaptive-capacity dynamics
- simplified failure-risk and institutional-learning indicators
- no empirical validation
- no legal, AI governance, public-sector, infrastructure, climate, risk, compliance, or strategic advice

Professional use requires qualified decision-science, AI governance, legal, compliance, public-interest, technical, ethics, audit, risk, stakeholder, and domain review.
