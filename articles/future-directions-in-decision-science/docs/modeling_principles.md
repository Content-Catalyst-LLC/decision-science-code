# Modeling Principles

## Future decision science is system design

Decision science should study the lifecycle of framing, evidence, modeling, values, authority, implementation, monitoring, correction, and learning.

## AI support requires governance

AI improves decision systems only when its role, uncertainty, validation, human oversight, and accountability are explicit.

## Robustness matters under deep uncertainty

Future decisions should be tested across plausible scenarios rather than optimized for one forecast.

## Reproducibility is accountability

Decision workflows should be rerunnable, inspectable, versioned, and connected to decision records.

## Ethics and power are part of method design

Values, equity, stakeholder standing, contestability, and remedy should be built into decision architecture.
