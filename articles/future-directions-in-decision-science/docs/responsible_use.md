# Responsible Use

These workflows are decision-support scaffolds.

Use them to:
- compare future decision-science pathways
- explore governance maturity
- test uncertainty and adaptive-capacity triggers
- model failure-risk dynamics
- structure decision records
- support teaching, discussion, and learning

Do not use them as a substitute for professional decision analysis, public governance, AI risk management, legal advice, compliance, audit, infrastructure review, climate planning, or institutional accountability.
