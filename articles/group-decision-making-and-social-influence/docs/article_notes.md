# Article Notes: Group Decision-Making and Social Influence

This article explains how teams, committees, expert panels, organizations, publics, and institutions make judgments when social interaction shapes evidence use, confidence, authority, dissent, consensus, and accountability.

Core themes:
- group decision-making
- social influence
- collective judgment
- hidden profiles
- information pooling
- groupthink
- authority bias
- conformity
- dissent
- consensus pressure
- decision rules
- expert panels
- AI-mediated decision support
- decision records
