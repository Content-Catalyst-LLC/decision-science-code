# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic group cases and stylized influence dynamics.

Limitations:
- synthetic estimates
- simplified expertise and status scores
- simplified influence weighting
- simplified hidden-profile metrics
- simplified consensus pressure
- no empirical validation
- no psychological assessment
- no legal, clinical, financial, engineering, policy, organizational, or safety advice

Professional use requires domain expertise, validated data, governance, human review, and careful interpretation.
