# Modeling Principles

## Heuristics are compression strategies

They reduce cognitive effort when full analysis is impossible.

## Bias is systematic distortion

Bias is not merely random error; it has direction and recurring structure.

## Context determines heuristic quality

A shortcut can be adaptive in one environment and misleading in another.

## Confidence must be calibrated

A judgment process should preserve probability estimates and compare them with outcomes.

## Debiasing is process design

Bias is reduced through structure, roles, records, feedback, and governance.
