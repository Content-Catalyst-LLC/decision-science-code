# Article Notes: Judgment Under Uncertainty

This article explains how people and organizations form beliefs, interpret evidence, make forecasts, express confidence, and choose actions when information is incomplete and outcomes remain unknown.

Core themes:
- uncertainty
- ambiguity
- risk
- belief formation
- base rates
- Bayesian updating
- likelihood ratios
- evidence quality
- heuristics
- anchoring
- overconfidence
- calibration
- Brier scoring
- expertise
- feedback
- decision records
