# Assumptions and Limitations

The workflows use synthetic judgment cases and stylized Bayesian-style updating.

Limitations:
- synthetic probabilities
- simplified likelihoods
- simplified confidence behavior
- simulated outcomes
- simplified evidence-quality categories
- no empirical validation
- no psychological assessment
- no legal, clinical, financial, engineering, policy, or safety advice

Professional use requires domain expertise, validated data, governance, human review, and careful interpretation.
