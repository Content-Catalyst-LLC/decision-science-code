# Lock-In

Lock-in occurs when a system becomes difficult to redirect because dependencies have accumulated around one path.

Types:
- technical lock-in
- economic lock-in
- institutional lock-in
- behavioral lock-in
- political lock-in
- infrastructure lock-in
- contractual lock-in
- data lock-in

Lock-in does not always mean change is impossible. It means change has become costly, contested, slow, or risky.
