# Article Notes: Resilience, Adaptation, and Long-Horizon Decisions

This article explains how decision-makers design strategies that remain viable across uncertainty, disruption, changing conditions, and extended time horizons.

Core themes:
- resilience capacity
- adaptation
- long-horizon decisions
- intertemporal trade-offs
- adaptive pathways
- threshold review
- shock absorption
- recovery capacity
- institutional memory
- intergenerational responsibility
- robust decision-making
- decision accountability
