# Article Notes: Risk Analysis and Probabilistic Reasoning

This article explains risk analysis and probabilistic reasoning as core practices in decision science.

Core themes:
- likelihood and consequence
- hazard, exposure, vulnerability, and response capacity
- probability quality
- expected loss
- variance and volatility
- value at risk
- conditional value at risk
- threshold breaches
- scenario stress testing
- Bayesian updating
- model risk
- risk communication
- decision records
