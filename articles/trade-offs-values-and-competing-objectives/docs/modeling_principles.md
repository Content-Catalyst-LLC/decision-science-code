# Modeling Principles

## Trade-offs reveal value structure

A trade-off shows what a decision-maker is willing to sacrifice to protect, maximize, or preserve something else.

## Weights are value judgments

Weights are not neutral. They express priorities and should be documented.

## Not all trade-offs are legitimate

Some objectives involve rights, safety, dignity, ecological thresholds, or legal obligations that should not be offset casually.

## Uncertainty changes trade-offs

A trade-off that looks acceptable under one future can become unacceptable under another.

## Records support accountability

Decision records preserve what was sacrificed, protected, assumed, contested, and selected before outcomes are known.
