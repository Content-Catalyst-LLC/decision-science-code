# Article Notes: What Is Decision Science?

Decision science is the interdisciplinary study of structured judgment under uncertainty, complexity, and competing objectives.

This article frames decision science as broader than formal decision theory. It includes mathematical models, behavioral insight, organizational process, evidence evaluation, systems awareness, and accountable governance.

Core themes:
- Decision framing
- Alternatives and objectives
- Probability and evidence
- Risk and uncertainty
- Expected value and expected utility
- Multi-criteria decision analysis
- Regret and robustness
- Sensitivity analysis
- Decision records
- Institutional learning
