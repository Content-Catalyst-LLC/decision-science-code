# Assumptions and Limitations

The synthetic examples in this directory are designed for reproducible decision-science demonstration.

They should not be interpreted as empirical findings. They illustrate decision workflows, not real-world recommendations.

Limitations:
- Synthetic data
- Simplified criteria
- Stylized scenario assumptions
- Simplified utility functions
- No stakeholder elicitation
- No domain-specific calibration
- No external validation

Professional use requires domain expertise, evidence review, stakeholder participation, and governance.
