# Article Notes: Why Uncertainty Changes Decision-Making

Uncertainty changes decision-making by weakening the assumptions of predictability, stable probabilities, complete outcome specification, and clean optimization.

Core themes:
- risk versus uncertainty
- ambiguity and unknown probability structure
- deep uncertainty
- limits of expected value
- cognitive constraints under uncertainty
- organizational uncertainty
- complex systems and feedback
- robustness and regret
- option value and reversibility
- adaptive pathways
- decision records
