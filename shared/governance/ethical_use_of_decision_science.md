# Ethical Use of Decision Science

Decision science should clarify judgment, not hide values behind technical language.

## Guardrails

- Define the decision clearly.
- Make alternatives visible.
- Document assumptions.
- Surface values and trade-offs.
- Include affected stakeholders where appropriate.
- Do not confuse model output with legitimacy.
- Represent uncertainty honestly.
- Preserve human accountability.
- Review decisions after outcomes emerge.
